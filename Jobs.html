Real Estate Agent

Reak Estate Agents buy, sell or rent properties for their clients. They must have a good knowledge of the real estate market and pricings of their communities and neighborhoods.They also find the best fitting properties for their clients. Based on what their looking for, what their price range are and what neighborhood they would like to live or buy in.

Qualifications

There aren't many requirments on being a real estate agent. The minimum requirments are that, you have to be 18 years or older, that you have to have a high school diploma and a real estate license for that state.

Salaries

Start off salaries for Real Estate agents depend on which company you work for, how much schooling, how many qualifications you have and where you are doing real estate in. The median pay for a real estate agent is $45,610 annualy. However salaries for real estate agents can range from anywhere between $23k-$105k.
